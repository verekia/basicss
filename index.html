There are a lot of places to get a gyro on Monroe St, but Golden Fleece seemed to be calling us. Literally. There were two dudes waiting for take out near the doorway telling us how great it was. Turns out these two dudes were right.

Just the best chocolate croissant and almond croissant I've eaten in my whole French life! The owners are a French couple and they also manage the San Francisco Bakery Institute.

Around this office this place is not known as Perilla. That's just it's mortal name. Instead it's better known by the mythical name that has been given to this business by Demeter, The Greek Goddess of Harvest, Bread and Grain.
